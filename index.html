About

Hi! I’m Lhen de Guzman and I am an aspiring data scientist.

Most recently I worked as a Research Analyst for Integreon Managed Solutions where I am mainly tasked to process corporate data into insights to serve as decision support tools for investors. During my tenure, I managed to deliver ad-hoc tasks with 98% metric score and automate repetitive tasks which resulted in the increase of my team’s productivity by 20%, as well being branded as the “Excel queen”.

Currently, I am a part of the Data Science program of FTW Foundation. It’s a 14-week immersive scholarship program for Data Science
and Executive skills training.

I am equipped with a Bachelor’s degree in Secondary Education major in Mathematics from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines - San Pedro Campus. I graduated as a member of the Student Council Association and the President of our College’s organization, Society of Math Educators, where I also served as a Treasurer in 2016. I was awarded as one of the Top 10 Debaters during the Intercollegiate debate in 2018, and our team managed to win 2nd place during the 2017 competition.

I also served as the Head of Research and Academics department for the Bread Society International Laguna Chapter where I helped in conceptualizing and organizing numerous seminars, alternative classroom learning experiences and symposiums. I was also given the opportunity to cover the 2016 National Elections for Radio La Verdad 1350 as one of their pioneer Student Reporters.
